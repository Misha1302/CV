Systems depth

Wist2

Modular .NET platform for DSLs and runtimes: frontend, bytecode/AIR, interpreter and CIL backend. The platform lets a .NET application accept restricted formulas and DSLs, validate them before execution, interpret them for diagnostics, and compile supported hot paths into typed delegates.

  • AIR interpreter and typed CIL generation through DynamicMethod.
  • Explicit module contracts and runtime selection without a hardcoded backend.
  • Semantic-equivalence checks across execution paths and cautious benchmark interpretation.
