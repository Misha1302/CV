Language tooling platform

Wist2

Modular .NET platform for DSLs and runtimes with a frontend pipeline, bytecode/AIR, interpreter and CIL backend. The platform lets a .NET application accept restricted formulas and DSLs, validate them before execution, interpret them for diagnostics, and compile supported hot paths into typed delegates.
