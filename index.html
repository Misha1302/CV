05

Research interests

Formal models and their connection to executable systems.

I am interested in problems where a formal model can be implemented, tested against real system behavior and evaluated under performance constraints.

01

Computational complexity

Classes P, NP, coNP and FP, polynomial reductions, completeness and the distinction between decision, search and function problems.

02

Formal languages & semantics

Automata, parsing theory, binding and normalization, and the relationship between syntax, semantics and execution.

03

Program analysis & testing

Exact and conservative reasoning, integer constraints, metamorphic and generative testing, and correctness and performance regression search.

04

Lambda calculus and foundations of type systems

Untyped and simply typed lambda calculus, normal forms and evaluation strategies, combinatory logic, fixed points, product, sum and recursive types, with introductory knowledge of polymorphic and dependent types.
