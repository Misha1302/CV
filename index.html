Flagship · Compiler/runtime framework

UniversalToolchain / Wist

A modular .NET framework for embedded languages with explicit semantic boundaries between frontend, Bytecode, AIR, optimizations and execution backends.

03 · Approach

Semantic boundaries

Frontend, IR and runtime have distinct responsibilities and do not create competing sources of truth.

Inference strength stays explicit

I separate exact, conservative and witness-based strategies so that “unknown” never becomes an unjustified “yes” or “no”.

Change without surprises

Deterministic composition, idempotency, compatibility, recovery and reproducible release gates.

05 · Research

Formal models and executable systems.

I am interested in problems where a formal model can be implemented, validated against real system behavior and compared with performance constraints.

01

Computational complexity

P, NP, coNP and FP, polynomial reductions, completeness, and the distinction between decision, search and function-computation problems.

02

Automata, parsing theory, binding and normalization, and the connection between syntax, semantics and execution.

03

Program analysis and testing

Exact and conservative methods, integer constraints, metamorphic and generative testing, and correctness/performance regression discovery.

Untyped and simply typed lambda calculus, normal forms and evaluation strategies, combinatory logic, fixed points, products, sums and recursive types; introductory familiarity with polymorphic and dependent types.
