Compiler/runtime platform

Wist2

Modular .NET platform for defining DSLs and executing programs: dialect definitions, lexer/parser/AST, bytecode/AIR, optimizers, an AIR interpreter and typed CIL generation through DynamicMethod. The platform lets a .NET application accept restricted formulas and DSLs, validate them before execution, interpret them for diagnostics, and compile supported hot paths into typed delegates.

IR direction

Callable-first SSA

An experimental Wist2 direction where operations are calls with attributes and SSA remains an optional IR layer.

  • Operations are modeled as callable methods with pure/commutative attributes.
  • Language semantics are separated from optimization representation; SSA remains an optional capability.
  • Lowering to intrinsics happens only when an explicit backend capability is available.
