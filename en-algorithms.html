AdvancedAlgorithms

Compiler/runtime SDK

Wist2

A modular .NET platform where algorithms operate inside a verifier-gated compiler/runtime pipeline rather than as isolated demonstrations.

  • AIR-to-SSA lowering and SSA-to-AIR emission with structural verification before and after every pass.
  • Local constant folding, SCCP-lite, branch folding, unreachable-block cleanup and dead pure-instruction elimination.
  • Capability-gated routing, controlled fallback and interpreter/CIL semantic-equivalence tests.
